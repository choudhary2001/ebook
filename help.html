Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I download an eBook?

Click the "Download eBook" button on any eBook details page. The file will be downloaded to your device automatically.

What file formats are available?

All eBooks are available in PDF format, which can be read on any device with a PDF reader.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, we only offer a web-based platform that works perfectly on mobile devices through your browser.

How do I search for eBooks?

Use the search bar on the homepage to search by title or author. You can also browse by categories.

Are the eBooks free?

Yes, all eBooks on our platform are completely free to download and read.

Can I read eBooks offline?

Yes, once downloaded, you can read the PDF files offline using any PDF reader application.
